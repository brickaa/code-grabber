Pull Quotes

There are two options for pull quotes, full-width or 50% width, floated right. You'll need to adjust the styles on the container, and use <blockquote> for 50% width/floated and a regular <p> on full-width. Otherwise, class names and styles are the same.

Float right, 50% width:

"Proin magna. Vestibulum rutrum, mi nec elementum vehicula, eros quam gravida nisl, id fringilla neque ante vel mi." — Person McPerson
